East West Institute of Technology was established in 2001 and is affiliated to Visvesvaraya Technological University, Belgaum. The Institute is approved by AICTE and accredited by NBA and NAAC. The Institute is famous for engineering as well as management courses and offers UG, PG and PhD programmes in numerous specializations. Admissions in these courses are based on entrance examinations.
